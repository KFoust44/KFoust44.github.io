Yin and Yang

Disclaimer: I came up with this idea at like 9:40 while watching the Avatar so there is a good chance this will be bad.

Yin and yang is an ancient Chinese Philosophy that brings about the idea that two opposites can be intertwined in such a way that they cannot be without one another. There is no good without bad. There is no life with out death. There is no sun without the moon.

Yin

Yin represents the negative energy of the two such as Darkness, Death,and the Moon.

Yang

Yang represents the postive energy of the two such as Light, Life, and the Sun.
